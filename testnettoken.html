About Jinwoo Token

Jinwoo Token (SUNG) is a payment-oriented token designed to enhance seamless transactions for businesses and platforms. By integrating with payment systems, we aim to revolutionize how transactions are done globally, bringing the power of cryptocurrency to everyday use.

Features

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000 (1 Million)
  • Payment Integration: Accept Jinwoo Token in your business or platform.
  • Mintable: Only by the owner, controlling token supply.
  • Burnable: Burnable by holders to decrease total supply.
  • Secure & Transparent: Built on the Polygon Blockchain, ensuring full transparency and security.
